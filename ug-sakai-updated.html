Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my Sakai password? +
Visit the UG ICT Help Desk portal at ict.ug.edu.gh and click "Forgot Password". You'll receive an email to your student UG mail. If you no longer have access to your UG email, visit the ICT Directorate in person with your student ID card.
How do I submit an assignment on Sakai? +
Navigate to your course, click the "Assignments" tab, then select the specific assignment. Click "Submit Assignment", attach your file (PDF, DOCX, or as specified by your lecturer), and hit "Submit". You'll receive a confirmation email.
Why can't I see my grades for a course? +
Grades are released by your lecturer and may not be available immediately after submission. If grades aren't showing after 3 weeks of submission, contact your course coordinator directly or raise a ticket through the Academic Registry.
How do I contact ICT Support? +
